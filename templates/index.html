Crowdsourcing Voice Research

The voice data is being collected for audio research. Each participant is required to submit his id (Full Name), birthdate (to estimate the age of the participant), and gender along with audio data. Make sure your microphone is working properly and you are not in a very noisy environment (A little background noise is okay but your voice should be intelligible). Make sure of the following while submitting the data:

  • • Fill up your full name, birthdate, and gender.
  • • Start recording, record for 40 sec. to 2 minutes with continuous speech.
  • • You can use multiple languages (e.g. Bangla, English, Hindi, Arabic). You can utter anything as long as they are valid sentences.
  • • Before submitting each record, make sure your voice can be understood without any difficulty by playing the recording. Finally, submit the recording.
  • • Record at least 4-5 audio recordings (each 40 sec. to 2 min). The more the better.
